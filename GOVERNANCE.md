# Governance

## Roles

- **Maintainers**: final review/merge rights and moderation.
- **Contributors**: anyone submitting issues, PRs, reviews, or docs.
- **Community mentors**: experienced contributors who guide newcomers.

## Decision process

- Small changes: maintainer consensus in PR.
- Major changes: open proposal issue + at least 2 maintainer approvals.

## Review expectations

- Initial maintainer response target: 5 business days.
- Reviews should be actionable and respectful.

## Moderation

Behavior is governed by `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`. Enforcement actions are at maintainer discretion.
