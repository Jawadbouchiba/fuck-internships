# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a harassment-free, inclusive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give feedback on work, not identity.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Help beginners learn.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Doxxing, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Dismissive gatekeeping behavior.

## Reporting

Report incidents to maintainers through private GitHub issue or maintainer contact listed in `docs/maintainers.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban users for violations.
